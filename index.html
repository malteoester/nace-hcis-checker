NACE Code High Climate Impact Sector Checker

Identify whether a NACE code is classified as a high climate impact sector under ESRS and SFDR reporting frameworks.

High climate impact sectors are defined as sectors listed in Sections A to H and Section L of Annex I to Regulation (EC) No 1893/2006. This classification is used in sustainability reporting under:

  • ESRS (European Sustainability Reporting Standards) – e.g., ESRS E1-5
  • SFDR (Sustainable Finance Disclosure Regulation) – e.g., Principal Adverse Impact Indicator #6

Use the tool below to check if a NACE code falls under the high climate impact sector classification.

Where can I find my NACE code? A NACE code is a numerical classification of economic activities. You can typically find it on official company registration documents or industry listings.
