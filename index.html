NACE Code High Climate Impact Sector (HCIS) Checker

Identify whether a NACE code is classified as a High Climate Impact Sector (HCIS) under ESRS and SFDR reporting frameworks.

High Climate Impact Sectors (HCIS) are defined as sectors listed in Sections A to H and Section L of Annex I to Regulation (EC) No 1893/2006. This classification is used in sustainability reporting under:

  • ESRS (European Sustainability Reporting Standards) – e.g., ESRS E1-5
  • SFDR (Sustainable Finance Disclosure Regulation) – e.g., Principal Adverse Impact Indicator #6
